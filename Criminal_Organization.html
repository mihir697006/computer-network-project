Criminal Organization Threats in Cybersecurity

What is a Criminal Organization Threat?

Criminal organization threats involve cyberattacks carried out by highly structured groups focused on financial gain, espionage, sabotage, or disruption. These groups often function like corporations, complete with leadership, funding, and division of roles. Some even collaborate with nation-states.
